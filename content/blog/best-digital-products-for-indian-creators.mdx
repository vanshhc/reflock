The Indian creator economy crossed ₹2,000 crore in 2025 — and the fastest-growing slice of that isn't brand deals. It's creators selling their own digital products directly to their audience.

Here's a breakdown of what's working.

## 1. Notion Templates

**Why they sell:** Notion has exploded in India among students and young professionals. A well-designed template for productivity, study planning, or business SOPs can sell thousands of copies with zero ongoing work.

**Who it's for:** Designers, productivity creators, founders, students.

**Typical price range:** ₹99 – ₹999

## 2. Paid Communities

**Why they sell:** People don't just want information — they want access, accountability, and belonging. A ₹499/month community around a niche (investing, fitness, freelancing) is the most scalable recurring-revenue product for creators.

**Who it's for:** Educators, coaches, niche experts.

**Typical price range:** ₹299 – ₹2,999/month

## 3. Cohort-Based Courses

**Why they sell:** Pre-recorded courses have a low completion rate and lower perceived value. Live cohorts create urgency, community, and results — which means buyers come back and refer others.

**Who it's for:** Skill teachers in writing, coding, design, finance, fitness.

**Typical price range:** ₹2,999 – ₹19,999 per cohort

## 4. AI Tools and Prompt Packs

**Why they sell:** As AI goes mainstream, professionals want shortcuts. A curated set of prompts for a specific use case (legal drafting, marketing copy, lesson planning) saves hours and sells for a premium.

**Who it's for:** Early AI adopters with domain expertise.

**Typical price range:** ₹499 – ₹4,999

## 5. 1-on-1 Coaching

**Why they sell:** High-touch, high-trust, high-price. Coaching is the fastest path to ₹1L/month for experts who already have a strong personal brand.

**Who it's for:** Career coaches, business mentors, fitness trainers, therapists.

**Typical price range:** ₹5,000 – ₹50,000+ per package

---

## Which one should you start with?

If you have time but limited audience → **Template** (low effort to create, tests product-market fit)

If you have an engaged audience → **Cohort course** (fastest path to ₹1L)

If you want long-term recurring revenue → **Paid community**

The best creators on platforms like re. often stack two or three of these — a free newsletter, a paid community, and an annual cohort.
